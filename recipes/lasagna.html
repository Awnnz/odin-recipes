Lasagna

Description heading

Perfect meal for dinner on the weekend. Quick to make and feeds a family of four!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix everything together in a bowl
  2. Pre heat Oven
  3. Bake for 30 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
