Cajun Chicken

Description heading

Perfect meal for dinner on the week nights. Quick to make and feeds a family of 2!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Lemon pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Mix everything together in a bowl
  2. Preheat grill
  3. Grill for 6-8 minutes
